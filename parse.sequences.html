Parse biological sequences (skbio.parse.sequences)¶

This module provides functions for parsing sequence files in a variety of different formats, the available parsers are listed below.

Functions¶

parse_clustal(record[, strict]) yields labels and sequences
parse_fasta(infile[, strict, label_to_name, ...]) yields label and seq from a fasta file.
parse_fastq(data[, strict, phred_offset]) yields label, seq, and qual from a fastq file.
parse_qual(infile[, full_header]) yields label and qual from a qual file.
